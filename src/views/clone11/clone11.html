Won Hundred presents:

DEADSTOCK

The Won Hundred deadstock collection consists of 5 unisex styles, including relaxed shirts, denim-inspired jackets, and pants. In an industry characterized by seasons, the deadstock collection has been produced with long-term partners during the factories' "low season" to maintain a stable workflow.

The fabrics are sourced from trusted partners in Portugal and are put together in Bulgaria using fabrics from the past eight Won Hundred collections. Furthermore, Won Hundred strives to secure an alignment with one of our Green Cross goals: increasing production made within the EU.

Each style is made from left-over materials, which gives each item a contrasting and different look. In addition, the use of deadstock fabrics offers the Won Hundred design team an opportunity to mix different colors and patterns in a new deconstructed way simply because of the limitations of the left-over materials.

The Won Hundred deadstock collection launches exclusively via wonhundred.com and is offered in limited quantities only.

Won Hundred presents:

A NEW TAKE ON LEATHER BOOTS

Together with legendary boot manufacturer Sendra, Won Hundred has designed and produced two signature silhouettes as part of the FW22 collection.

Made in 100% leather, the boots are rooted in our denim universe and combined with our fashion aesthetic, making these boots a perfect fit for every occasion. They are available in two colorways, black and brown, the boots offer a versatile feel and look for everyone.

For FW22, Won Hundred launches 1 pair of womenswear boots and 1 unisex silhouette for both men and women.

Produced in partnership with Sendra

In close partnership with Sendra, we have been working and developing this unique silhouette to accommodate our vision from a design perspective. With more than 100 years in the business, Sendra has played a key part in making this vision come to life.

Today, Sendra serves as an industry leader when it comes to manufacturing and production of leather boots, and through Sendra’s strong sense of quality and creation of long-lasting products – the company has won recognition worldwide.

OUR SUSTAINABILITY AMBITIONS

At Won Hundred, we take a proactive stance in bettering our industry and reducing our environmental impact. We believe the key to ethical and responsible business practices is not only reducing our carbon footprint but also improving the conditions for the people and animals involved. It is important for us that our suppliers wish to partake in our journey - only this way can we achieve long-term success and trust.

We are aware that the fashion and textile industry holds many challenges in terms of ethics and sustainability. However, as sustainability applies to everybody, it is our belief that the size of the company should never be an excuse to practice sustainable and responsible business. We are convinced that continuously “doing something” is better than “doing nothing”.
